CallRemoteSDPChanged

The remote call party has sent us a new SDP. Re-INVITE requests are used to change the SDP and it can indicate a range of conditions such as:

  • Changing the RTP end point, e.g. a B2B server wants to reinvite iteslf out of the media path,
  • Call is being aplced on or off hold,
  • Codecs changed in response to bandwidth conditions,
  • And more. Applies to calls initiated by us and calls recevied by us.
